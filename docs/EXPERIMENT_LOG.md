## Historical Direct AD

The historical direct-AD trajectory sweep is preserved under
`results/raw_logs/direct_ad/`. It contains ten standalone logs for
`alpha = 0.2` and `alpha = 0.8` at `500`, `1000`, `2000`, `5000`, and `10000`
AD steps.

These runs were generated by `mhw_jax.py`, whose direct-AD objective returns
`xp.sum(metrics)`. The raw objective and raw gradients are preserved in
`results/summary/tables/direct_ad_window_sweep.csv`, along with normalized
per-step values.

The historical sweep started from configured initial conditions and did not use
the later detached saturated-state spin-up workflow.
